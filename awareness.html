Floods

Heavy rainfall and river overflow cause devastating floods across India, especially during monsoon season.

Causes

  • Heavy rainfall
  • River overflow
  • Dam failure
  • Cyclone-induced surges

Prevention

  • Build embankments
  • Improve drainage
  • Plant trees
  • Early warning systems

Before Flood

  • Prepare emergency kit
  • Know evacuation routes
  • Secure property
  • Stay informed

During Flood

  • Move to higher ground
  • Avoid walking in floodwater
  • Follow authorities
  • Conserve food/water

After Flood

  • Check for injuries
  • Avoid contaminated water
  • Document damage
  • Seek help if needed

Earthquakes

Sudden ground shaking caused by tectonic movements, common in Himalayan and western regions.

Causes

  • Tectonic plate movement
  • Fault lines
  • Volcanic activity

Prevention

  • Build earthquake-resistant structures
  • Regular drills
  • Secure heavy objects

Before Earthquake

  • Prepare emergency kit
  • Know safe spots
  • Secure home
  • Family communication plan

During Earthquake

  • Drop, Cover, Hold On
  • Stay away from windows
  • Don't use elevators

After Earthquake

  • Check for injuries
  • Inspect for damage
  • Avoid damaged buildings
  • Follow aftershocks

Cyclones

Powerful tropical storms that affect coastal regions with high winds and heavy rain.

Causes

  • Low pressure systems
  • Warm ocean temperatures
  • Atmospheric conditions

Prevention

  • Build shelters
  • Strengthen infrastructure
  • Early warning systems
  • Coastal protection

Before Cyclone

  • Stock emergency supplies
  • Know evacuation routes
  • Secure property
  • Stay updated

During Cyclone

  • Stay indoors
  • Avoid windows
  • Follow evacuation orders
  • Conserve resources

After Cyclone

  • Assess damage
  • Avoid downed power lines
  • Check for contamination
  • Seek medical help

Droughts

Prolonged dry periods leading to water scarcity and agricultural losses.

Causes

  • Low rainfall
  • Climate change
  • Deforestation
  • Over-extraction of water

Prevention

  • Water conservation
  • Rainwater harvesting
  • Drought-resistant crops
  • Sustainable practices

Before Drought

  • Store water
  • Conserve resources
  • Plan for reduced yields
  • Monitor weather

During Drought

  • Ration water
  • Protect livestock
  • Seek government aid
  • Conserve energy

After Drought

  • Replenish water sources
  • Restore agriculture
  • Learn from experience
  • Implement long-term solutions

Landslides

Mass movement of rock, earth, or debris down a slope, common in hilly regions.

Causes

  • Heavy rain
  • Earthquakes
  • Deforestation
  • Mining activities

Prevention

  • Plant trees
  • Build retaining walls
  • Proper drainage
  • Land use planning

Before Landslide

  • Know risk areas
  • Prepare emergency kit
  • Monitor weather
  • Have evacuation plan

During Landslide

  • Move to safe area
  • Avoid landslide path
  • Stay alert
  • Follow warnings

After Landslide

  • Search for survivors
  • Avoid unstable areas
  • Report incidents
  • Seek professional help

Cloudbursts

Sudden, intense rainfall over a small area, causing flash floods in mountainous regions.

Causes

  • Intense rainfall
  • Mountainous terrain
  • Atmospheric instability

Prevention

  • Weather monitoring
  • Early warning systems
  • Infrastructure protection

Before Cloudburst

  • Know flash flood risks
  • Prepare emergency kit
  • Stay informed
  • Secure belongings

During Cloudburst

  • Move to higher ground
  • Avoid low-lying areas
  • Follow evacuation orders

After Cloudburst

  • Check for injuries
  • Inspect damage
  • Avoid contaminated water
  • Report to authorities

Heatwaves

Prolonged periods of excessively hot weather that can cause health emergencies.

Causes

  • High temperatures
  • Humidity
  • Climate change
  • Urban heat islands

Prevention

  • Heat action plans
  • Public awareness
  • Cooling centers
  • Water availability

Before Heatwave

  • Stay hydrated
  • Avoid outdoor work
  • Check on vulnerable people
  • Prepare home cooling

During Heatwave

  • Stay indoors
  • Drink plenty of water
  • Wear light clothing
  • Avoid alcohol/caffeine

After Heatwave

  • Monitor health
  • Rehydrate slowly
  • Check on others
  • Learn preventive measures
