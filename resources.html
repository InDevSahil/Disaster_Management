Government Schemes & Programs

National Cyclone Risk Mitigation Programme (NCRMP)

Aims to reduce vulnerability of coastal communities to cyclones through early warning systems, cyclone shelters, and capacity building.

Disaster Mitigation Projects

Funding for structural and non-structural measures to mitigate disasters like floods, landslides, and earthquakes.

PM Relief Fund

Provides immediate relief to victims of natural calamities through financial assistance and essential supplies.

National Disaster Response Fund (NDRF)

Provides funds for emergency response, relief, and rehabilitation in case of calamities of severe nature.

State Disaster Response Fund (SDRF)

Assists states in meeting expenditure for providing immediate relief to victims of natural disasters.

Flood Management Programme

Comprehensive program for flood control, drainage improvement, and anti-erosion measures.

Drought Mitigation Programs

Initiatives for water conservation, drought-resistant agriculture, and livelihood support in drought-prone areas.

Earthquake Risk Reduction Programs

Retrofit of buildings, awareness campaigns, and development of earthquake-resistant construction technologies.
